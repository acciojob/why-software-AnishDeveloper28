Why I want to become a developer?

Being a software developer means having the ability to bring ideas to life, improve people's lives through innovative solutions, and contribute to the ever-evolving tech landscape. It's a field that challenges me intellectually and creatively, and I am passionate about being part of this dynamic and impactful profession.
